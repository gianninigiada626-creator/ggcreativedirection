Giada Giannini is a Creative Director specializing in AI-driven visual storytelling. With a foundation in photography and a deep understanding of brand culture, she builds campaigns that carry a thesis — not just an aesthetic.

Her process starts with brand analysis and cultural positioning — understanding not just what a brand sells, but what it means.

Based in Italy. Available worldwide.
